Tourist Visa FAQs

Answers to the most common tourist visa questions.

How long does a tourist visa take to process?

+

Processing times vary by country. UAE e-Visa takes 24â€“72 hours. Schengen visas take 15â€“30 days. UK tourist visas take 3â€“8 weeks. US B-2 visas can take 2â€“6 months depending on the interview slot. We always recommend applying well in advance.

What is a Schengen visa and which countries does it cover?

+

A Schengen visa allows you to travel freely across 27 European countries including France, Germany, Italy, Spain, Netherlands, Switzerland, and more. You apply through the embassy of the country you'll spend the most time in or enter first.

Can I extend my tourist visa once I'm abroad?

+

Extension policies vary by country. Some countries like UAE and Thailand allow extensions, while others like the UK and Schengen area generally do not allow extensions for tourist visas. We advise applying for the appropriate duration from the start.

Do I need a travel agent to apply for a tourist visa?

+

While not mandatory, working with a consultant significantly reduces the risk of rejection. We ensure your documents are complete, your financial proof is presented correctly, and your application tells a compelling story. Our 98% success rate speaks for itself.

What if my tourist visa is rejected?

+

We analyze the rejection reason, address the issues, and help you reapply with a stronger application. For countries like the UK and US, we also prepare you for any required interviews. A rejection doesn't mean the end of your travel plans.
